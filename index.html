The boring stuff

This website help you memorize new words that you learn today, you only need to do, is put the english text on question section and spanish for the answer section, and We will give you a random test based on the question you provide to us.

Step 1: Add your questions and answers

On a dashboard with a + button. Click on it to add a new question and answer pair. You can enter any English word or phrase as the question and its Spanish equivalent as the answer. For example: question = Hello answer = Hola You can add as many questions and answers as you want. The more you add, the more you can practice.

Step 2: Take a random test

When you are ready to test your knowledge, click on the Test button on the dashboard. You will see a random question from your list. Try to type the correct answer in Spanish. If you are not sure, you can click on the Hint button to see the first letter of the answer. If you are still stuck, you can click on the Show Answer button to reveal the answer.

You will get feedback on whether your answer is correct or not. You will also see your score and progress on the dashboard. You can take as many tests as you want. The questions will be shuffled each time.

Step 3: Review your results and learn from your mistakes

After each test, you can review your results and see which questions you got right and which ones you got wrong. You can also see the correct answers and compare them with your own. This will help you learn from your mistakes and improve your vocabulary.

You can also click on the History button on the dashboard to see your past tests and scores. You can track your improvement over time and see how much you have learned.

Step 4: Share your feedback and suggestions

We hope you enjoy using this website and find it useful. If you have any feedback or suggestions on how to make it better, please let us know. You can click on the Contact button on the footer of the website and send us a message. We appreciate your input and we will try to implement your ideas.
